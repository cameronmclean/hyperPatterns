Laboratory patterns

A framework to communicate and share expert knowledge about lab procedures

Design patterns present a novel knowledge representation framework for capturing expert knowledge about complex problems.

Here we extend the concept of design patterns into the domain of the laboratory and provide a service for publishing design patterns in the form of structured, linked open data to connect more of our laboratory knowledge on the web. This site is intended to facilitate the capture and structuring of knowledge following a pattern mining workshop.

Use the buttons above to navigate between patterns. You can view exisiting patterns or create, edit, and publish new laboratory patterns to share with the world. Note that when editing existing proto-patterns, any associated file attachments will remain unchanged unless you specify a new file to replace them. Click here for a short screencast introducing the basic ideas and function of this site.

Once published, patterns can no longer be changed and are made available as linked data under the URI scheme described here.
